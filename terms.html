Terms & Service Policies

Please read our terms carefully regarding our digital services.

01

Scope of Work


Bagground provides services based strictly on the scope discussed and agreed upon at the time of purchase or written confirmation.

Any requests that fall outside the agreed scope will be treated as additional work and may require extra time and charges. If something is out of scope, it will always be communicated clearly before proceeding.
02

Post-Delivery Changes


Projects are delivered as per the agreed scope and approvals.

Any content updates, fixes, or changes requested after final delivery are handled as one-off paid work, unless the client has an active Monthly Support plan.

For clients who anticipate frequent updates, a Monthly Support plan is recommended for better value and faster turnaround.
03

Revisions Policy


Each project includes up to two rounds of revisions during the active build phase.

Revisions apply only to adjustments within the existing structure, layout, and provided content.

New ideas, additional sections, or major changes introduced after approvals are considered outside the original scope and may be billed separately.
04

Payment & Invoicing


Full payment (or the agreed advance) is required before work begins.

Any one-off update or additional work must be paid before execution.

Invoices are issued as non-GST invoices, as Bagground operates as a sole proprietorship where GST registration is not applicable.

All invoices are issued digitally and must be cleared within the timeline mentioned on the invoice.
05

Timelines & Delays


Project timelines are shared as estimates and depend on timely inputs, approvals, and feedback from the client.

Delays in providing content, feedback, or approvals may result in adjusted delivery timelines.
06

Content Responsibility


Clients are responsible for providing accurate text, images, and business information.

Bagground is not responsible for errors, omissions, or issues arising from incorrect or incomplete content supplied by the client.

Copywriting beyond basic structuring, formatting, or light refinement may be offered as an additional paid service if required.
07

Ownership & Usage


Once full payment is received, the client owns the final website output created for them.

Bagground reserves the right to showcase completed projects in its portfolio, social media, or marketing materials unless the client requests otherwise in writing.

All internal design systems, frameworks, and reusable templates remain the intellectual property of Bagground.
08

Refund & Cancellation


Once work has started, payments are non-refundable.

If a project is canceled mid-way, charges will apply based on the amount of work completed up to that point.

Monthly Support subscriptions can be canceled at any time and do not auto-renew unless explicitly stated.

Communication


Official communication is handled via email or WhatsApp.

Support requests are addressed during standard business hours.

Priority response and faster turnaround are available only for clients with an active support plan.
